OUR STORY

Our journey began at the University of Santo Tomas, where coffee fueled our late nights, study sessions, and countless conversations. What started as a simple habit grew into a passion — not just for coffee itself, but for the connections, inspiration, and comfort it brings to people’s lives.

From those moments, the idea for Mundaze Coffee was born: a rustic, student-founded café built on creativity, community, and the joy of sharing a warm cup. Today, every brew we serve carries the spirit of UST — hard work, friendship, and the belief that coffee is best enjoyed together.
